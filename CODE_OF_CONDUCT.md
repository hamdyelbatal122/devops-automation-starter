# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression,
level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and inclusive
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and learning from mistakes

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insults, and personal attacks.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.
They may remove comments, commits, or contributions that violate this code.
